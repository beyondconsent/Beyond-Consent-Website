Our Story

Bridging Legal Complexity with Survivor Empowerment

Beyond Consent was born from a critical need to bridge the gap between legal complexity and survivor empowerment. Our mission is to make legal knowledge accessible to those who need it most, while building a supportive community for survivors of digital harassment and consent violations.

In a world where digital harassment is increasingly common, survivors often face complex legal systems without proper guidance. We believe that knowledge is power, and everyone deserves to understand their rights and options.

Educational Focus

Breaking down complex legal concepts into understandable, actionable guidance

Sarah Johnson, JD

Host & Legal Advocate

Boston, MA

Sarah is a practicing attorney specializing in digital rights and privacy law. With over 8 years of experience in victim advocacy, she brings both legal expertise and compassionate understanding to every episode.

Her work has been featured in major legal publications, and she has testified before Congress on digital consent legislation. Sarah is passionate about making legal knowledge accessible to those who need it most.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting in touch

How quickly do you respond to messages?

We typically respond to general inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Confidential submissions may take up to 48 hours to ensure proper care and attention.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All communications are handled with strict confidentiality. We never share personal information without explicit consent, and our confidential form uses additional security measures.

Can I be a guest on the podcast?

We welcome guest inquiries! Please use our general contact form and select "Guest Appearance" as your inquiry type. Include information about your expertise and story.
